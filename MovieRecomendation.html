The Best Movies According to Preethi

My Top 3 Movies all-time

3 Idiots

It's an Popular Hindi Language film
It tells about the story of 3 engineering students. I really like the phrase "ALL IS WELL" which is from the movie.

Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl

It tells the life story of the only women in the air force who participated in the kargil war. It tells about how her dreams of flying an airplane made her landed in air force.
It's such a motivating story which is telling us that never give up your dreams!

Kalki 2898 AD

It's a SCI-FI action film
It's such an story like mixing elements which are present in the Hindu books and making a story out of it. It gave me goosebumps! Must watch movie!
